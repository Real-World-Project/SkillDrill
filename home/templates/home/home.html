About

Find Out More About Us

SkillDrill is an online marketplace for local services where services like Repair & maintenance, home cleaning, homecare & design, pest control, packers & movers, business services, event management, weddings & party management, health & wellness, salon, and other local services are all available on SkillDrill. It helps customers to book reliable home services like plumbing, carpentry, painting and so on. It creates job opportunity for millions of jobless people. Its vision is to empower service professionals to deliver services at home like never seen before. Its main motto is to make people hassle free from wandering for service professionals.
